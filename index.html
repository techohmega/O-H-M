Services and Offerings

We provide comprehensive supply and services solutions to a wide range of industries, utilities, infrastructure customers, and contractors.

Engineering Studies:

Our team conducts engineering studies encompassing various aspects, including: Power system network studies, Network modelling, CT (Current Transformer) and VT (Voltage Transformer) sizing, Transformer sizing, load assessments, Busbar sizing, Relay setting calculations and coordination studies, Battery and battery charger sizing, Cable sizing, and more.

Engineering Documentation:

We specialize in the preparation of engineering drawings, schematics, and general arrangement plans. We configure engineering solutions for relays, network switches, SAS (Substation Automation System), and RTU (Remote Terminal Unit) automation units.

Testing and Commissioning:

Our experienced team performs testing and commissioning services for a range of equipment from leading manufacturers, including ABB, Hitachi, Siemens, GE, and Schneider. This includes protection relays, SAS/Scada automation, RTUs, PLCs, VLCs, and DCS systems.

Erection and Installation:

We offer services for the erection and installation of various equipment, including control, protection, and automation systems. This covers GIS (Gas-Insulated Switchgear), LCC (Local control cubicle), LVAC (Low-Voltage Alternating Current) panels, and more.

Cable Services:

Our cable-related services include cable laying, cable termination, and establishing interfaces between control, protection, SAS, LCC, and AVR/RTCC panels.

Panel Supply:

We provide a wide range of panels for control, protection, LCC, RTCC, AVR, SAS, RTU, VLC, and PLC applications. also we supply 11kV Switchgear.

Accessories:

We also offer various accessories, including test switches, test plugs, and auxiliary relays.

Safety Equipment:

Additionally, we supply safety equipment, safety shoes, and coveralls to ensure a safe working environment.

These services and offerings reflect our commitment to delivering comprehensive solutions to meet the needs of our clients in the industry, utility, and infrastructure sectors.
